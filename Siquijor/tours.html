Siquijor Tourist Spots

Cambugahay Falls

Located in the town of Lazi, Cambugahay Falls Siquijor is considered as one of the most beautiful waterfalls in the Philippines. This Siquijor falls is a sure-hit for tourists because of its turquoise clean and warm water originating from natural springs. Its water will invite you to plunge in, the moment you see it.

Cantabon Cave

If you think Siquijor Island is just about magic and mystery, and falls and beaches, well, it’s more than that. Head to Cantabon Cave to discover the subterranean beauty of the province where you can see many stalactites, stalagmites and rock formations that will stir your imagination.




Old Enchanted Balete Tree

As with many Siquijor tourist attractions, the Old Enchanted Balete Tree has a mystical and spiritual background. This 400-year old tree is said to be used for sacred rituals by the local shamen. What makes the Balete Tree stand out is that there’s a fishpond at the tree’s base where you can dip your toes.




Paliton Beach

Even though Paliton Beach is hidden, travelers manage to find this beach to enjoy a piece of paradise. Once you’re there, you’ll be welcomed by fine white sand and turquoise waters.




Siquijor-Butterfly Sanctuary

Located in Larena town, the 200 sqm. Siquijor Butterfly Sanctuary will let you interact with over 20 species of butterflies. From caterpillar to butterfly, you’ll see them in every stage of life. It’s not only a tourist attraction, but it also serves as an educational conservation center that teaches school children about biodiversity and how to preserve it. This is definitely one of the best places to visit in Siquijor.

Salagdoong Beach

Even with man-made structures at Salagdoong Beach Siquijor, it maintained its natural charm thanks to the rock formations surrounding it. This beach is perfect for picture-taking, chilling, and cliff jumping.




San Juan Beach

Another famous Siquijor tourist attraction is San Juan Beach. It has fine white sand and cerulean waters perfect for swimming. There are also many resorts lining San Juan Siquijor Beach where you can spend a whole afternoon of cold drinks.




St. Francis of Assisi Church

St. Francis De Assisi Church was established on February 1, 1783. It was administered by secular priests on February 1, 1783 and the construction of this stone church was initiated by P. Setten. The convent was built to be a hiding place from pirates.




Siquijor Hotels



  • Suite Princess Beach Resort

    Set in Siquijor, 2 km from Talisay Beach, Suite Princess Beach Resort offers accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a bar. With a shared lounge, the property also features a garden, as well as a private beach area. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, a shared kitchen and currency exchange for guests. The hotel offers a continental or à la carte breakfast. Suite Princess Beach Resort offers 5-star accommodation with a hot tub and barbecue. You can play darts at the accommodation.

  • Golden Sunset Inn

    Boasting a bar, GOLDEN SUNSET INN is set in Siquijor in the Visayas region, 1.6 mi from Pontod Beach. Featuring a restaurant, the 4-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom. The hotel has family rooms. Extra beds are dependent on the room you choose.

  • White Villas Resort

    White Villas Resort is a small, cosy resort on Solangon Beach, good for families, groups, couples and singles. It has a nice restaurant by the beach, free wifi, small swimming pool, sunset beach bar, free kayaks, and offer motortbike rental and island tours by car. It is also only 15 minutes from Siquijor Town ferry terminal and 10 minutes from the new Siquijor Airport, and provide free pick-up transfer.

  • Bermuda Triangle Bungalows

    Located in Siquijor, 0.7 mi from Tubod Beach, Bermuda Triangle Bungalows features accommodations with a garden, free private parking and a terrace. The property provides a 24-hour front desk and free WiFi is available throughout the property. The rooms are fitted with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fridge, a kettle, a shower and a desk. At the resort rooms have a wardrobe and a private bathroom.

  • Garden Bungalows Resort

    Spend some days at the beautiful and quiet place of Garden Bungalows Resort. Enjoy the swimming pool, the jacuzzi and relax on the lounge beds, the rocking chairs below the old tree or on the bench at the pergola. The best place to unwind and the same time the base to discover the island, visit the places of interest or take a trip for snorkeling, diving, kayak or mountain bike. Nearest beaches are 10 minutes (by motor). They have only 7 rooms, new and comfortable, each with it's own terrace. Mission: To provide highly personalized service and total satisfaction to guests making them want to come again. How to get there: - Boats from Dumaguete - 5 minutes from the port (by motor) - free pickup on call

  • Mandala Tribe Treehouses

    Located in Siquijor, 0.9 mi from Pontod Beach, Mandala Tribe Treehouses provides accommodations with a terrace and free private parking. With a garden, the 3-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom. The rooms have a balcony with a garden view. At the hotel, each room is equipped with a wardrobe. Guest rooms at Mandala Tribe Treehouses are equipped with a seating area. Guests at the accommodations can enjoy a à la carte or an American breakfast.
